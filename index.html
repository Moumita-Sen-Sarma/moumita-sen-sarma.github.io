About Me

Hello, I'm Moumita Sen Sarma. I have completed B.Sc in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) from Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET). My research interests are computer vision, machine learning, deep learning and data science. I have also served as a chairperson of IEEE CUET WIE AG Student Branch & membership coordinator of IEEE Student Branch CUET and organized some skill enriching events. I believe I'm creative and a quick learner with an analytical mind. I worked as Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at East Delta University, Bangladesh from 19.09.2021 to 16.07.2023. Currently, I'm working as a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at my Alma Mater, Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET), Bangladesh.
